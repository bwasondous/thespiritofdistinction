Our Story

Born From The Warmth
of Haitian Tradition

Bwason Dous — meaning "sweet drinks" in Haitian Creole, merges deep Caribbean traditions with contemporary innovation. The focus has always been centered on producing premium, soulful beverages crafted specifically for the most discerning palates.

We are introducing Kremas, our flagship offering, to the world on our terms—safeguarded by our family crest, which serves as the very insignia of our company. It's a new kind of drinking experience. From its texture to its flavor, Kremas introduces a fresh perspective: elegant, unexpected, and entirely its own. This isn’t a reinvention of Kremas, it's an official arrival.

This artisanal beverage has graced Haitian tables since our country’s independence in 1804, carried through generations as a symbol of celebration and resilience. We do not claim its origin—only the honor of having perfected its taste. Kremas by Bwason Dous is the most well-balanced, and premium Kremas ever produced. What we offer is a smooth, graceful expression of Kremas, crafted with care and presented in its most polished form — finally brought to the world by The St. Vilûs & Louis Family.
