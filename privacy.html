Privacy Policy

01 — Introduction

Bwason Dous ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website at bwasondous.com. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you interact with our site.

By visiting our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any of its terms, please discontinue use of this site.

02 — Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us. This may include:

  • Email Address — When you join our reservation or newsletter list.
  • Age Verification — A timestamp confirming that you have represented yourself as 21 years of age or older. No date of birth is stored.
  • Usage Data — Anonymous data such as browser type, pages viewed, and time spent on site, collected via standard analytics tools.

We do not collect sensitive personal data such as financial information, government-issued identification, or health records.

03 — How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To notify you when Kremas becomes available for purchase or release.
  • To send occasional updates, announcements, and invitations to private events.
  • To maintain legal age-verification compliance for an alcohol-related website.
  • To analyze site performance and improve the user experience.

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

04 — Cookies & Local Storage

Our site uses browser local storage to temporarily record your age verification session. This data expires automatically and is not transmitted to any external server.

We may also use cookies from analytics providers to understand aggregate visitor behavior. These cookies do not identify you personally. You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting your ability to browse this site.

05 — Data Retention

We retain your email address on our mailing list until you choose to unsubscribe. Every communication we send includes an unsubscribe link. Upon request, we will delete your data from our records within 30 days.

06 — Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party service providers to manage our email list or host our website. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for any purpose other than providing services to Bwason Dous.

Our site may load external fonts from Google Fonts. Google's privacy policy governs how they handle any data associated with those requests.

07 — Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent to email communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@bwasondous.com.

08 — Children's Privacy

This website is intended exclusively for individuals who are 21 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone under the legal drinking age. If we become aware that a minor has submitted information to us, we will delete it immediately.

09 — Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of this site after any modification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
